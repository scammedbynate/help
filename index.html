Stop Nate Stark

The 18-year-old serial scammer from Sunny Isles. Operating with impunity. We are exposing the truth.

The Individuals Involved

Nate Stark is an 18-year-old resident of Sunny Isles Beach, FL, who has become notorious for orchestrating schemes involving fake event tickets, undelivered luxury goods, and fraudulent loans.

He does not operate alone. Reports consistently identify his brother as a key accomplice, assisting in the collection of funds and the management of these deceptive operations.

The Legal Shield

Victims seeking justice often face intimidation. It is alleged that Nate's mother is a lawyer who actively intervenes to protect her sons. Victims report receiving legal threats instead of refunds, allowing the duo to continue their activities without immediate consequence.

Evidence & Messages

Real screenshots of conversations, transactions, and fake promises.

How They Steal

Fake Tickets

Selling invalid tickets to high-demand events. When victims arrive at the venue, the tickets scan as fake or already used.

Luxury Goods Scam

Taking deposits for expensive colognes or designer items. Once payment is sent via Zelle, communication stops.

The "Loan" Scheme

Asking for short-term loans for "business deals" with promises of high returns. The money is never repaid.
